Dr. Sung-Hee Kim

Bio

Dr. Sung-Hee (Sonny) Kim is a professor and Associate Chair of Civil Engineering at the University of Georgia. He specializes in resilient infrastructure and pavements, pursuing teaching and research to advance sustainable, resilient infrastructure management systems using nondestructive testing, remote sensing, and machine learning algorithms. Over the past decade, Dr. Kim has been awarded over $10 million in research grants and has published more than 150 refereed journal articles, proceedings, and technical reports. His honors include ASCE Fellow, Distinguished Faculty Fellow by UGA College of Engineering, 2021 ASCE Civil Engineer of the Year, and 2021 Georgia Engineer of the Year by the Georgia Society of Professional Engineers.
