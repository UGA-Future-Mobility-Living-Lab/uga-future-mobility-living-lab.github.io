Latest updates from the Future Mobility Living Lab

Autonomous Vehicle Research Featured for High School Campers

June 19, 2025

On June 19, 2025, the Future Mobility Living Lab hosted a special session showcasing its autonomous vehicle research to high school students participating in a summer camp. The event offered students a hands-on introduction to emerging technologies in transportation.

PhD Students Present Research at ASCE ICTD 2025

June 8-12, 2025

From June 8–12, 2025, PhD students Wenjie Zhao, Zhipeng Bao, and Tianle Zhu presented three research papers at the International Conference on Transportation and Development (ASCE ICTD) held in Arizona. Their work highlights the cutting-edge research being conducted at the Future Mobility Living Lab.

Future Mobility Living Lab Explores Autonomous Transit Innovation with Beep, Inc.

May 5–7, 2025

From May 5–7, the Future Mobility Living Lab at the University of Georgia joined the Georgia Network for Electric Mobility on a site visit to Beep, Inc., a leading provider of autonomous mobility solutions. Beep, in collaboration with HOLON and Mobileye, is addressing the complex challenge of deploying autonomous last-mile services and transit connectors in real-world environments. Throughout the visit, participants observed active deployments, engaged in technical discussions, and explored potential areas for future collaboration.
